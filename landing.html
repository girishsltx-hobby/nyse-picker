Capabilities

A complete intraday toolkit — from raw price data to AI-generated trade signals.

📡

Real-Time WebSocket Feed

Prices, indicators, and AI signals update every 5 minutes via persistent WebSocket. Auto-reconnects on disconnect. Zero page refreshes needed.

🤖

AI UP / DOWN Predictions

27-rule weighted evaluator scores EMA, VWAP, volume, candle structure, ORB, and S/R signals. Returns a directional prediction with confidence score, per-rule explanations, and optional LLM plain-English commentary.

📊

Candlestick Chart + Overlays

Lightweight Charts v5 with session background shading (pre-market, regular, after-hours), ORB, VWAP, PM/PD price lines, and swing markers.

16+ Technical Indicators

EMA 9/21/50 with spread %, RSI-14 (Wilder), RVOL / volume state, VWAP position & motion, daily trend, POC, ORB 15m, premarket H/L, previous day H/L, support & resistance, swing H/L, and a 9-signal confluence score.

Dynamic Watchlist

Five tiered pattern detectors (T1–T3): POWER_TREND, STRUCTURE_BREAK, VWAP_RESET, EXHAUSTION_REVERSAL, and POC_MAGNET. Alerts are batch-displayed per refresh cycle with tier filters and a manual ↻ Refresh button. Deduped within 15 minutes.

Architecture

How it works

Five focused layers, each doing one job well.

1

Data Fetch

yfinance pulls 5-day / 5-min OHLCV bars every 5 minutes for every tracked ticker.

2

Indicators

Python computes EMA, RSI-14, RVOL, VWAP, trend, ORB, session levels, S/R, volume profile, EMA spread, and 9-signal confluence score in-process.

3

AI + Composite Alerts

33-rule engine fires UP/DOWN + confidence. 5 composite detectors check for POWER_TREND, STRUCTURE_BREAK, VWAP_RESET, EXHAUSTION_REVERSAL, and POC_MAGNET patterns.

4

Broadcast

FastAPI WebSocket pushes price, indicator delta, and prediction to all connected clients instantly.

5

Visualise

React + Zustand merges the stream into local state. Charts and panels update live, no polling.

Composite Alert Patterns

High-conviction setups, explained

Five tiered composite detectors fire only when multiple indicator conditions align simultaneously — no noise, just high-probability setups.

⇕ T1 · POWER_TREND

Power Trend

Full EMA stack aligned (9>21>50 or 9<21<50), price on the correct side of VWAP, and daily trend confirming direction. The highest-conviction setup in the system.

⇕ T1 · STRUCTURE_BREAK

Structure Break

Price breaks through a key support or resistance level with volume confirmation. Signals a potential trend continuation or acceleration after the break.

⇕ T2 · VWAP_RESET

VWAP Reset

Price reclaims or loses VWAP with EMA structure confirming direction. Institutions repositioning around fair value — typically kicks off a directional move.

⇕ T2 · EXHAUSTION_REVERSAL

Exhaustion Reversal

RSI extreme (overbought / oversold) with price near a swing high/low and volume drying up. Momentum is exhausted — fade the move and expect a reversal.

⇕ T3 · POC_MAGNET

POC Magnet

Price is trading away from the session Point of Control (highest-volume node). The POC acts as a gravitational anchor — expect price to revert toward it over the next few bars.

↑↓ 33-RULE AI ENGINE

Per-bar Predictions

Every 5-minute bar also runs the full 33-rule weighted evaluator: EMA stack, VWAP position & motion, RSI, momentum, candle structure, ORB, S/R, and confluence — scoring an UP/DOWN with confidence.
